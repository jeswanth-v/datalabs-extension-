This service may handle the text you send but does not store or use it beyond providing translations. 4. Data Security We ensure that no personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not track user activity. 5. Your Choices Since our extension does not store or track data, there is no need for additional privacy controls. If you have concerns, you can disable or remove the extension at any time. 6.
